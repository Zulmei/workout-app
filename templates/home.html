Log As You Lift

Users can use the website to log their workout in real time as they are working out in the gym. Track your sets, reps, and weights effortlessly to stay on top of your fitness journey.

Track Your Progress

Users can utilize the workout history and calendar features to track their progress over time. See how far you've come, identify trends, and stay motivated by visualizing your fitness journey.

Choose Your Workout Plan

Users can choose from a library of different workout plans that are already made for them and their goals. Whether you're looking to build muscle, increase endurance, or maintain a healthy lifestyle, there's a plan tailored for you.

Create Your Own Plan

Users can create their own workout plans to suit their unique fitness needs. Customize exercises, reps, and sets to build a plan that helps you achieve your personal fitness goals.
